THESSALONIKI ARCHAEOLOGICAL MUSEUM: 60 YEARS | 60 MOMENTS
On October 27, 1962, the city grandly inaugurated its new archaeological museum. This year, 60 years later, we are honoring this important anniversary with an exhibition that illuminates 60 emblematic moments of the historical path of the largest and oldest archaeological museum in northern Greece. The guided tour will be conducted by the archaeologist Katerina Behtsis, with the presentation of the entrance ticket.
At the end of the tour, the participants will have the opportunity to get to know the "New acquisitions / new approaches": New interpretations of old finds from ancient Olynthos. The ten-minute presentation will be made by Mrs. Evangelia Tsagaraki, head of the Ceramics, Metalworking & Micro-Crafts Department of AMTH.
